📋 Frequently Asked Questions

How do I request time off? ▼

Go to Time Management → Leave Requests and submit a new request with your dates and reason.

How do I access my paystubs? ▼

Visit Compensation → Paystubs to view and download your recent paystubs.

What benefits am I eligible for? ▼

Check Compensation → Benefits to see your current benefits and coverage details.

How do I submit an IT ticket? ▼

Click "IT Support" above or visit the IT Portal to submit technical issues and requests.
